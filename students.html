    It is worth noting that Dr. Abolfazl Eslami has had a significant impact on the education and training of the next generation of geotechnical engineers. As a professor at Amirkabir University of Technology, he has supervised a large number of graduate students, including 32 Ph.D. students and 152 Master's students. Dr. Eslami's mentoring has undoubtedly contributed to the development of these students' knowledge and skills in geotechnical engineering. His guidance and support have helped them to conduct innovative research, publish scholarly articles, and make valuable contributions to the field. The knowledge and experience gained under his mentorship have prepared these students for successful careers in academia, industry, and other related fields. Overall, Dr. Eslami's mentorship of a large number of graduate students underscores his commitment to the education and training of the next generation of geotechnical engineers. His legacy will undoubtedly continue through the many students who have benefited from his guidance and expertise.
