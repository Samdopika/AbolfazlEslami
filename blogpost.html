    Winter 2023

    I am Professor Abolfazl Eslami, a guest professor at UC San Diego's Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering. I'm collaborating with Professor McCartney to develop techniques for designing structures that can withstand extreme events. It's been an incredible experience working with Professor McCartney and the talented students at UC San Diego. I'm grateful for the opportunity and excited to continue pushing the boundaries of structural engineering.
